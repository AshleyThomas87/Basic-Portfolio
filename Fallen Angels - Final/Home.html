Mission Statement



Fallen Angels is a non-profit organization built on second chances and putting people first. We provide essential resources for formerly incarcerated men and women. Our mission is to help individuals transition back into soceity, address challenges, form meaningful relationships, pursue higher educational goals, and strengthen confidence in all people so they can be productive members of society.
