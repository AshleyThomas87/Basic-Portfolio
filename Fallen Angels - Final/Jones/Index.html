We have several options available if you need help with utilities such as electric, gas and water. These are the most important utilities you need to survive and keep yourself and family healthy. SRP, APS, and your local City Water & Gas Companies will wave the down payments and offer monthly discounts for members of their program.

We know that having a phone and internet service is an essential part of our everyday lives, but they can be a costly expense. Being a new releasee with so much on your plate, let us help you with a phone and internet. You will need these services to contact new employers, continue education, and pay bills. We have partnered with T-Mobile, AT&T, Verizon and Sprint, and these service providers agree with our mission and would like to help.

Sometimes we all need a safe place to go to, and a fresh start in the right direction, but where do we start? We have many listings of State Certified and approved halfway houses in your area. These houses are designed to help you get on your feet, and you will be surrounded with people who genuinely care and want to help. These programs can help with 1st month's rent and provide substance abuse counseling, and assistance finding employment while you are there. Many of these homes have different variety of plans, such as 3, 6, or 12 month stays.

Are you looking for an apartment to rent or a home to buy, but cannot afford the steep rent, mortgage, or down payments? Does the idea of all that paperwork seem daunting? We can direct you to a Single Parent Program available for our qualifed Fallen Angels who have custody of their children under 18 years of age. In addition to having 100's of listings of discount homes in your area, this program will assign a counselor who will help with all of the paperwork that comes with renting or buying.

In addition to low-cost housing resources, these Single Parent Programs can also assist with Child Care for all of our Fallen Angels who need to get back to work. We have partnered with experienced and trustworthy, State Certified Child Care Providers.
