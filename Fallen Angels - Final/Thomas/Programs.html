Employment opportunities are a lot better today for those who have a record. Many companies are willing to hire felons. Yes they get a tax credit, but at the same time you're getting a second chance to be viewed for your work ethic and not your past/charge. You can work in any industry you see fit to yourself. Whether it be fast food, grocery stories, or even large companies like Nike, Amazon, etc. Goodwill is able to help you with job placement, resumes, and interview preperation. If you're good with computers and into the tech world many companies are turning to working remotely due to Covid-19.

There are a lot of Programs you can attend for various reasons located all across the valley. You can recieve In-Patient and Out-Patient Rehab Treatment. Attend AA, NA, CA meetings just to name a few. For some individuals 12 Steps is a huge part of their lives and recovery. SAGE counseling also covers information about addictitions and the coping skills you'll need to stay on the right path. If you're a parent and need to take classes those too are available. You can check out our Child Care, Housing & Utilities page. If we don't have something listed you can refer to are comment box on the home page and make a request for a specific class. Fallen Angels will look into finding what ever request and get you that information and location.

Education is very important to individuals. There are many opportunities these days to further your education. FAFSA is able to help with grants and student loans. It is now easier to attend classes from home with Online Courses. There are Trade Schools also refered to as boot camps that range from 6 months to 18 months. Then there is always Community College or regular college. You can attend day or night at any age in any career. Rio Salado helps those who haven't gotten their High School Diaploma study and pass exams to recieve their GED. We're here to help you succeed and further your education. To see you accomplish your dreams and goals.
